Pizza

Description

This is an easy-to-follow recipe for authentic Napoli-style pizza in your own home, no wood-fire oven needed.

Ingredients Needed

  • 2 Cups of All Purpose Flour (plus an additional 1/4 cup)
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1 packet of Quickstart Yeast
  • 3 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1/2 tsp Sugar
  • 14 oz, Can of Crushed Tomatoes
  • 8 oz Fresh part-skim Mozzerella
  • 4 oz Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Sift and combine ingredients and Olive Oil.
  2. Slowly add 2 cups of warm water while mixing dough slowly
  3. Add remaining flour if dough gets too sticky. You'll know when its right when it no longer sticks to the side of the bowl.
  4. Remove dough from the mixing bowl and divide into two even balls.
  5. Cover the dough balls with a cloth and let rest for 30 minutes.
  6. Punch down dough and place in the fridge for at least 1 hour, but up to 48.
  7. When ready to cook, remove dough from fridge and allow to come up to room temperature. Preheat oven to 500F
  8. Lightly oil your pan and roll dough out to about a 12 inch circle
  9. Puree crushed tomatoes and spread evenly over the pizza dough.
  10. Spread both cheeses evenly over the sauce, and bake for 10-12 minutes.
  11. Enjoy!
